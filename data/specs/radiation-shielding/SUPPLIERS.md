# Radiation Shielding Supply Chain Landscape

This guide profiles commercial suppliers—particularly SMEs and specialist teams—that provide materials, tools, or software relevant to radiation shielding for crewed Mars mission concepts. Companies are grouped by primary operating region to align with international program partnerships. Each entry highlights core capabilities and representative mission heritage or contracts where available (status current to late 2024).

---

## North America

### Nanocomp Technologies (Huntsman Advanced Materials) – Merrimack, NH, USA
- **Profile:** Pioneer in continuous carbon nanotube (CNT) sheets, tapes, and yarns; acquired by Huntsman in 2018 but retains SME-style production cell.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Low areal-density CNT laminates and emerging boron nitride nanotube (BNNT) products that combine structural reinforcement with secondary radiation attenuation.
- **Space Heritage:** NASA NextSTEP habitat trials for multifunctional shielding panels; supplied CNT materials for Orion EFT-1 test articles.
- **Notes:** Offers custom layups compatible with water-wall or polymer matrices; scalable roll-to-roll process aids large surface coverage.

### BNNT Materials (BNNT LLC) – Newport News, VA, USA
- **Profile:** Spin-off from Jefferson Lab producing high-purity boron nitride nanotubes.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** BNNT fibers and powders tailored for composite reinforcement; high hydrogen-equivalent content and neutron capture potential.
- **Programs:** Supported NASA RadWorks materials research and DARPA-funded evaluations for multifunctional shielding.
- **Notes:** One of few commercial BNNT suppliers with kilogram-scale output suited to structural composite fabrication.

### Radiation Shield Technologies (RST) – Miami, FL, USA
- **Profile:** Private company specializing in Demron® lightweight radiation shielding fabrics.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Hydrogen-rich textile laminates and garments adaptable to habitat liners, EVA overlays, and storm-shelter curtains.
- **Mission Experience:** Supplied material for NASA’s water-wall shelter prototypes and ISS-based crew protective garments.
- **Notes:** Provides rapid prototyping for bespoke soft-goods shielding solutions; supports sterilization and reusability requirements.

### Redwire Space (including former Techshot assets) – Jacksonville, FL, USA
- **Profile:** Mid-sized space infrastructure company with strong additive manufacturing and habitat technology portfolio.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Multi-purpose chassis that integrate water-wall bladders, 3D-printed regolith binders, and conformal shielding structures.
- **Programs:** NASA Exploration Extravehicular Mobility Unit (xEMU) habitat studies; ESA/Thales lunar shelter demonstrators; ongoing E3P proposals.
- **Notes:** Combines materials processing know-how (Out-of-Earth manufacturing) with structural integration—valuable for in-situ regolith shielding.

### Atmospheric & Space Technology Research Associates (ASTRA LLC) – Boulder, CO, USA
- **Profile:** SME delivering space weather modeling and radiation environment services.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Offers operational space-weather nowcasts/forecasts, radiation belt models, and SPE risk analytics that feed mission shielding and storm-shelter planning.
- **Customers:** NOAA SWPC, NASA Gateway program partners, commercial crew providers.
- **Notes:** Provides APIs and dashboard tools; useful for integrating real-time alerts with dosimetry systems.

### Bubble Technology Industries (BTI) – Chalk River, ON, Canada
- **Profile:** Canadian SME specializing in neutron detection and radiation instrumentation.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Bubble detector (BD) dosimeters used for neutron monitoring in ISS DOSIS/DOSIS-3D campaigns; ruggedized neutron spectrometers for surface habitats.
- **Programs:** CSA Health Beyond initiative, NASA SRAG support, ESA Matroshka collaborations.
- **Notes:** Offers calibration services and turnkey dosimetry kits for flight and analog environments.

### Radiation Solutions Inc. (RSI) – Mississauga, ON, Canada
- **Profile:** Provides gamma-ray and neutron detection systems with roots in mining and security.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Compact scintillator-based spectrometers adaptable to spacecraft radiation monitoring and surface rover payloads.
- **Heritage:** Delivered instrumentation for CSA and DOE programs; exploring integration with Gateway environmental packages.
- **Notes:** Modular electronics suited to instrumenting logistics modules or regolith processing plants.

## Europe

### LIQUIFER Systems Group (LSG) – Vienna, Austria
- **Profile:** Design consultancy and SME focusing on crewed habitat concepts and multifunctional structures.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Developed Water-Wall concepts, flexible shielding bladders, and regolith-settled habitat layouts under ESA Discovery & Preparation contracts.
- **Programs:** ESA “Water Walls” study (2018–2021), RegoLight regolith construction consortium.
- **Notes:** Offers concept-to-prototype services bridging industrial design and space systems engineering; ideal for integrating shielding within interior architecture.

### High Performance Space Structure Systems (HPS GmbH) – Munich, Germany
- **Profile:** SME specializing in deployable structures and satellite subsystems.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** DeShield® and FlexShield® multi-layer insulation (MLI) products that combine micrometeoroid protection with enhanced radiation attenuation.
- **Heritage:** ESA missions (Proba-3, MTG) and E3P habitat demonstrators; GSTP-funded shielding maturation.
- **Notes:** Provides flight-qualified laminates and integration support for large-area habitat surfaces.

### cosine measurement systems – Leiden, Netherlands
- **Profile:** Medium-sized company delivering advanced optical and X-ray detector systems.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Co-developer of Timepix-based DOSIS 3D detectors and miniaturized radiation spectrometers for ESA/ISS.
- **Programs:** ISS DOSIS-3D, ESA radiation environment monitoring on Gateway and lunar missions.
- **Notes:** Supplies instrumentation and data services enabling high-resolution dosimetry mapping within habitats.

### Arktis Radiation Detectors – Zurich, Switzerland
- **Profile:** SME delivering helium-free neutron and gamma detection systems.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Boron-10 and lithium-based detector modules suitable for monitoring secondary neutron fields around regolith-shielded habitats or storm shelters.
- **Customers:** ESA technology demonstrators, security agencies, CERN collaborations.
- **Notes:** Offers ruggedized units with low power draw—advantageous for continuous habitat monitoring.

### SUPERSHIELD Consortium (CINI / University of Pisa spin-out) – Pisa, Italy
- **Profile:** Academic-enterprise cluster funded under ESA OSIP for superconducting active shielding.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Conceptual design of MgB₂-based superconducting coils delivering localized magnetic shielding fields.
- **Status:** Completed OSIP “Kick-Start” feasibility; seeking GSTP follow-on for cryogenic system maturation.
- **Notes:** Early TRL, but one of the few European teams advancing hybrid active-passive shielding hardware.

### COMEX SA – Marseille, France
- **Profile:** Engineering SME with expertise in extreme environment structures (hyperbaric, subsea, space).
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Contributes to regolith excavation concepts, pressure hull reinforcement, and shielding logistics for habitats (RegoLight, EUROHAB).
- **Programs:** ESA regolith construction studies; partners with LSG/HPS on shielding demonstrators.
- **Notes:** Practical know-how transferring terrestrial heavy-engineering techniques to robotic regolith handling.

## Asia-Pacific

### Teijin Limited – Tokyo, Japan
- **Profile:** Advanced materials company partnered with JAXA via J-SPARC for space composites.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Aramid (Twaron®) and ultra-high-molecular-weight polyethylene (Endumax®) laminates optimized for combined MMOD and radiation shielding.
- **Programs:** Gateway I-HAB interior shielding studies; Lunar Cruiser (Toyota-JAXA) protective panels.
- **Notes:** Offers tailored textile architectures and resin systems compatible with water barrier integration.

### Toray Advanced Composites – Tokyo, Japan / Morgan Hill, CA, USA
- **Profile:** Produces high-performance composite prepregs and thermoplastics with global manufacturing.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Supplies carbon fiber/thermoplastic laminates and hydrogen-rich thermoplastics (PEEK blends) with spaceflight qualifications.
- **Heritage:** Habitual supplier to JAXA, NASA, and ESA spacecraft; exploring regolith-compatible binding matrices.
- **Notes:** Provides material data packages and processing support critical for certifying structural shielding components.

### Beijing Institute of Aeronautical Materials (BIAM) – Beijing, China (AVIC subsidiary)
- **Profile:** State-affiliated research institute with semi-commercial operations.
- **Shielding Capabilities:** Development of BNNT-enhanced polymers, boron-loaded resins, and hydrogenated composites for CNSA/CMSA missions.
- **Programs:** National Key R&D “Advanced Space Radiation Protection Materials”; partner in Tiangong station upgrades.
- **Notes:** While state-linked, BIAM offers contract manufacturing and materials testing services for domestic commercial space ventures.

## How to Use This List

1. **Material Down-Selection:** Pair hydrogen-rich or BNNT-enhanced materials (Nanocomp, BNNT LLC, Teijin, Mitsui) with structural requirements in `system-spec/DESIGN_APPROACH.md`.
2. **Storm Shelter and Habitat Integration:** Engage SMEs like LSG, HPS, Redwire, and RST for modular water-wall, soft-goods, or MLI-based shielding.
3. **Dosimetry & Monitoring:** Utilize BTI, RSI, cosine measurement systems, and Arktis to instrument habitats and logistics assets.
4. **Radiation Modeling & Forecasting:** Incorporate ASTRA services into mission operations per `system-spec/ANALYSIS_MODELING.md` and `OPERATIONS_SUSTAINMENT.md`.
5. **Regional Partnerships:** Align suppliers with agency procurement pathways (NASA NextSTEP, ESA GSTP/E3P, JAXA J-SPARC, ISRO RESPOND) referenced elsewhere in this repository.

> *This list emphasizes agile suppliers and research-driven SMEs complementing larger aerospace primes. Update periodically as new OSIP/NASA/CSA solicitations bring additional entrants into the shielding ecosystem.*
